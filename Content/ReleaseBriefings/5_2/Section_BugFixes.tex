\begin{frame}[fragile]{Atomics violated C++ semantics}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Our atomic operations violated C++ semantics when calling \texttt{atomic\_load} on a \texttt{const}
  \item Specifically our implmentation of \texttt{atomic\_load} and \texttt{atomic\_store} via \texttt{atomic\_compare\_and\_swap}
  \item Now we require \texttt{atomic\_load}, \texttt{atomic\_store}, and \texttt{atomic\_compare\_and\_swap} to be lock-free a specific \texttt{sizeof(T)} for all atomics to be lock free.
  \item Most relevant architectures provide all three for 4 and 8 bytes but only \texttt{atomic\_compare\_and\_swap} for 16 bytes.
\end{itemize}

\end{frame}
